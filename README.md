# Flappy Star
Flappy Star is a starry twist on the classic Flappy Bird. You guide a glowing star through a series of floating pipes in space.

## How to Play

* Press **Start** to begin playing. 
* Tap or press **Space** to make the star jump.
* Avoid hitting pipes or the ground/ceiling.
* Every pipe you pass increases your score.
* When the game ends, choose **Restart** or view the **Leaderboard**.

## Features

* Star trail particle system.
* Parallax background stars with streaking shooting stars.
* Clean UI overlays with animated buttons.
* Local leaderboard storing the top 5 scores.
